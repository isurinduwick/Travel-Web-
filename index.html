Popular Destinations

Explore the most beautiful and interesting places in Sri Lanka.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

★★★★★

Ancient rock fortress with stunning views and fascinating history.

Kandy

★★★★★

Cultural capital with the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic.

Ella

★★★★☆

Mountain village with panoramic views and hiking trails.

Galle Fort

★★★★★

Historic Dutch fort with colonial charm and ocean views.

Mirissa

★★★★☆

Stunning beaches and whale watching opportunities.

Yala National Park

★★★★★

Wildlife sanctuary famous for leopards and elephants.

Anuradhapura

★★★★☆

Ancient city with sacred stupas and historic ruins.

★★★★☆

Breathtaking waterfall nestled in the lush hills of Ella.
